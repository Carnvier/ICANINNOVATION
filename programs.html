Program Overview

The Hub offers a range of social impact programs designed to address critical needs and drive positive change. Our programs are categorized into five main lots, each focusing on a specific area of impact.

Lot 1: Community Development & Related Activities

  • Participatory community planning
  • Capacity building programs
  • Community engagement frameworks
  • Impact assessment and monitoring
  • Criteria: Focus on community empowerment and development.

Lot 2: Education & Skills Development

  • STEM education programs
  • Vocational training
  • Talent development workshops
  • Technical skills assessment
  • Educational technology integration
  • Criteria: Focus on education and skill development.

Lot 3: Entrepreneurship Development

  • Digital entrepreneurship training
  • Start-up incubation programs
  • SME development support
  • Criteria: Focus on entrepreneurship and innovation.

Lot 4: Development & Planning Support Services

  • Strategic planning support
  • Business growth consulting
  • Market analysis and research
  • Operational efficiency improvement
  • Criteria: Focus on advisory services.

Lot 5: Monitoring & Evaluation Support Services

  • Data collection and analysis
  • Performance monitoring systems
  • Impact evaluation frameworks
  • Data management solutions
  • Criteria: Focus on accountability and data-driven decision-making.
